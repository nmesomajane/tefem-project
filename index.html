WELCOME TO THE MUCHIGUN SCHOOL

The MUCHIGUN School aims at investing in the Nigerian girl child for the good of the society. We have both primary and secondary sections. Our school has a reputation of high moral and academic standards. We have been able to achieve these through our mission: ‘partnership with the parents to give an all-round education to the students, based on the dignity of the human person, integrity, leadership qualities and academic excellence’ and our vision: ‘Christian Identity’.

READ MORE FROM EZE PRECIOUS | THE HEAD TEACHER

MISSION

Partnership with parents to give an all-round education to each student, based on Christian principles, with emphasis on the dignity of the human person, integrity, leadership qualities and academic excellence.

VISION

To see every Lagoon student equipped with an integral education which enables her to play her unique role in the home, work place and the larger society.

CORE VALUE

Freedom with responsibility Dignity of work Responsible use of resources Spirit of service Parents involvement as primary educators
